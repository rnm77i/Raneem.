About Us

Traction has been one of Dubai's foremost luxury automobile dealers since the past decade and have been successful in consistently providing an extensive choice of new and used luxury cars at the most competitive prices to meet the needs of our diverse clients. No one understands luxury cars and their owners as we do; with their varied interests and needs in world renowned brands, they are always looking for the best. At traction , we mostly deal with new and used luxury cars and offer them at the most competitive market prices. With our long-standing professional relationships with auto brands from around the world, we are able to source the latest and most desirable high-end vehicles and offer them to our prestigious clients. We work with each and every one of our clients to source the best brands and models that will satisfy their individual requirements. We take pride in providing world-class customer service, before, during and after the sale or purchase of luxury cars. To serve our clients better, we have also expanded our business to Vehicle Trade-in and Trade for Cash, specializing in buying, trade-in and consignment. This gives our clients to enjoy a quick, smart an easy opportunity to part ways with their existing vehicle for a newer model or the best value in money.
